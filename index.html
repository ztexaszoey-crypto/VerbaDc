AP Chemistry Problem
How many grams of NaCl are needed to make a 2.0 M solution in 500 mL of water?
VerbaDoc's Solution:
  1. Molarity formula: M = moles/volume(L)
  2. Rearrange: moles = M × L = 2.0 × 0.5 = 1.0 mol NaCl
  3. Molar mass of NaCl: 23 + 35.5 = 58.5 g/mol
  4. Calculate mass: 1.0 mol × 58.5 g/mol = 58.5 grams
